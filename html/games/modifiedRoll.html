Modified Roll

Engine: Unreal Engine 5

Genre: Role Playing / Adventure

Still in Early Development.

Intended as an Open Source Project.

Add a 3rd dimention to your old tabletop adventures. As a GM (Game Master), create adventures for your friends to play through together in this top down role playing game.

Drag and drop mechanics for easy placement of scenery, containers, loot and characters. Dialog scripting with NPCs your characters can interact with, or as the GM take control of the NPCs and role play interaction or fights. Fights in the game are turn based with dice rolls just like the tabletop games you're used to running.

Custom mod support so you can bring in your own assets or characters to your adventure.
