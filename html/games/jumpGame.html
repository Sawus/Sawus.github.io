Jump Game

Engine: Unreal Engine 5

Genre: Platformer

Project designed to learn mobile game development with Unreal Engine.

Game is playable on both iOS and Windows.

Fun and challenging platforming game. Play as a slime traversing the world who can only move by jumping. Control the angle and power of you jump to navigate throught obstacles to reach the end of the level. As a slime, you can stick to almost any surface in the game. Use jump pads and conveyor belts and other mechanics to help you accomplish your goal.

Fully functional menu and pause system with level selector so you can choose which level to play or replay.
