Tear Devil

Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Genre: Action / Beat'em Up

George Brown College - Game Programming (T163)

Position: Technical Director

Lead Team of 4 Programmers

Play as a "Comic Book Villain" on a mission to cause as much destruction as possible. You can smash and throw anything you can find inside while either avoiding the security team or taking them on head to head.

Unfortunately lacking many animations as well as sound as we did not have an animator or sound designer for the project.
