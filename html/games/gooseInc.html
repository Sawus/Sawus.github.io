Goose Inc.

Engine: Unreal Engine 5

Genre: Action / Horror

Submission for TOJam2023

A group of friends are put through a series of deadly trials.

This game is a third person action game set in a dark underground facility. Experiments are being conducted on the characters and it is your job to keep them alive. The game is viewed from the perspective of security watching from a control room.
