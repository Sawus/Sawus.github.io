Isometric Strategy

Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Genre: Strategy

Project designed to learn 2D art and development withen Unreal Engine, as well as apthfinding algorithms such as A*.

Move units based on their movement distance and turn order. If multiple character's turn orders on the same team are sequential, you can click on either the portraits or the characters themselves to choose who should move first. Custom tile map generator created to take map height and untraversable tiles into consideration for movement of units.
